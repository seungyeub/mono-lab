# Meltdown Studios

A bold and energetic brand identity for an independent game development studio.

## The Approach

Meltdown Studios needed something that felt inherently creative but professional enough to attract tier-1 investors. The solution was a dynamic visual system that can adapt to their constantly changing creative output.

## Execution

* Vibrant color palette
* Responsive logo system
* Motion-first brand guidelines
